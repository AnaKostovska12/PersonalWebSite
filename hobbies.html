Cooking: My Passion

Cooking for me is more than just preparing meals — it’s a dynamic form of self-expression and a gateway to cultural exploration.Whether I’m experimenting with bold spices, crafting delicate pastries, or baking my signature cookies to share at college, food becomes a universal language that breaks barriers and sparks genuine connections.

Bringing homemade treats like cookies to campus isn’t just about sharing something sweet — it’s about fostering community and creating moments of joy amidst busy days. Cooking daily grounds me in mindfulness and creativity, transforming routine into ritual. It’s my way of nurturing both myself and those around me, proving that food has the unique power to unite diverse people, celebrate cultures, and make life’s moments more vibrant and meaningful.

Fashion, Thrifting & Aesthetic Design

Fashion and design are a big part of how I express myself creatively. I enjoy putting together outfits through thrifting, exploring unique styles, and playing with visual aesthetics. Whether it's assembling a look or designing something digital, I’m drawn to the details that make things visually impactful and meaningful. This passion naturally blends with my interest in creating clean, thoughtful designs in other areas of my life — especially in tech and beyond.
